HS64 Optical Stimulator¶

Authors

Jie (Jack) Zhang & Jonathan P. Newman

IO

Register Access

ONIX ID

5

ONIX Hubs

Headstage-64

Description¶

The HS64 Optical Stimulator is a two-channel high-current LED/laser diode driver for optogenetic stimulation (On Semi CAT4016). The maximal current can be set over a wide (~10 mA to 800 mA) range via an external digital potentiometer. The optical power can then be adjusted linearly and synchronously across both channels within this range over 8-levels per diode load. This sub-circuit is controlled as a single device using parameters similar to a Master8 or PulsePal.
